Arduino-Watch

This is my little 7 segment arduino-watch. The goal was to make a watch with a very low power consumtion.

Hardware

It's based on a ATmega328PU, a single CR2032-cell, some buttons, some capacitors, a resistor and a 4-digit 7-segment-display like this.

Low-Power

I use a standalone Arduino with a watch-crystal. I disabled the brown-out-detector in the bootloader for a lower powerconsumtion. Because of the modified bootloader, the watch has a powerconsumtion of around 1.2µA in sleepmode. The standard booloader has a powerconsumtion of about 16µA.
